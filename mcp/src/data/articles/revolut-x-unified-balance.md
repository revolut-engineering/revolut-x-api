# About Crypto Unified Balance

Your existing crypto balance will now be unified across both the main Revolut app and Revolut X. This means that you'll see a single crypto balance across both platforms.

All your open orders will be cancelled on the day of the migration before your balance is consolidated, and your portfolio analytics may take up to 1 day to update after the migration. We'll email you with the migration date.

Unified balances remove the need for topping up or withdrawing crypto assets from your main account to your X account, and vice versa. For tokens not supported on Revolut X, the balance will be visible, but will not be available for trading on Revolut X.

## What Happens to My Fiat Balance on Revolut X?

There will be a dedicated fiat currency balance in your Revolut X account, which you can use exclusively for Revolut X orders. You can top up, withdraw, and transfer this balance, similar to your main Revolut app fiat currency balance.

If you wish, you can also transfer your fiat currency balance to or from your main Revolut account.

## Do I Need to Take Any Action for the Unified Balance Setup?

The unification of the crypto balance across Revolut X and your main Revolut account will be automatic. You'll see the updated balance in both of the platforms, once the unified balance setup has officially launched.

## Can I Generate a Statement for Only Revolut X Transactions?

With the launch of a unified crypto balance, any transaction statements generated in Revolut X or the main Revolut app will have transaction histories from both platforms by default.

If you need a statement exclusively for Revolut X or your main Revolut account transactions, it can be generated on demand. Reach out to our support team and they will help you get this statement.
