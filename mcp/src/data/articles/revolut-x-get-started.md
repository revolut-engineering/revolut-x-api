# Get Started with Revolut X

## What Is Revolut X?

Revolut X is our cryptocurrency trading platform.

## How Do I Use Revolut X?

There are 2 ways to join Revolut X. If you already have a Revolut account, just follow these steps:

1. Go to the [Revolut X platform](https://exchange.revolut.com/home) or the Revolut X app
2. Log in with your Revolut account details. A pop-up will ask you to open your Revolut account to enable access to Revolut X
3. Accept the [Revolut X T&Cs](https://www.revolut.com/legal/crypto-exchange-terms/)
4. Answer a few questions about yourself and the purpose of your account to start using Revolut X

If you don't have a Revolut account, you can still sign up to Revolut X directly using the Revolut X mobile app.

## Who Can Use Revolut X?

- You must be at least 18 years old
- You must complete the sign-up process and have your documents verified by our team

To open and use a Revolut X account, you'll need to provide:

- A valid mobile phone number
- A valid email address
- Personal information, including but not limited to your address, date of birth, full name, and citizenship(s)
- A valid government-issued ID for identity verification
- A selfie for account security

## Revolut X Fees

Revolut X fees vary based on whether your order is a maker (placement in order book) or taker (immediate execution of order). Details on fees can be found on our [Crypto Exchange Fees](https://www.revolut.com/legal/crypto-exchange-fees/) page, or within the **'Trades'** section in your **'Activity'** tab after an order is filled.
