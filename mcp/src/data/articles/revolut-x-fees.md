# Revolut X Fees

Revolut X has different fees applicable to different functionalities of the platform.

## Revolut X Trading Fees

We offer exposure to cryptocurrencies trading. The exchange rate for your transaction is determined by the type of your order, which you can see on our [Trading Rules](https://www.revolut.com/legal/crypto-exchange-trading-rules/) page. To see how much fees you will be paying, see our [Fees](https://www.revolut.com/legal/crypto-exchange-fees/) page.

## Revolut X Withdrawal Fees

When you withdraw cryptocurrency externally in your Revolut X, you'll be charged a fee made up of 2 parts.

- **Revolut service fee:** 1 GBP (or equivalent in local currency) for XRP, XLM, DOT, SOL, AVAX, XTZ, ALGO, and ADA, or 3 GBP (or equivalent in local currency) for all other cryptocurrencies
- **Network fee:** The network fee is paid to the blockchain miners or validators who process the transaction

The network fee is variable and depends on the transaction speed and the traffic on the blockchain network. Revolut calculates the network cost, adds the service fee, and displays the total estimated cost. You'll never be charged more than what you see in the app.
