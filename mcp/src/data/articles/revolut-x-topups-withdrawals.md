# Revolut X Top-ups and Withdrawals

## Send and Receive Money

Currently, your Revolut X account supports sending and receiving funds from your personal Revolut account and crypto withdrawals to external addresses in the web terminal. We're actively working on adding deposits from external wallets.

## Top Up or Withdraw on the Web Platform

1. Tap **'Deposit'** or **'Withdrawal'** in the top-right corner
2. For withdrawals, pick the Revolut X balance matching the currency you want to withdraw. For top-ups, choose the balance from your retail app in crypto, or add USD, EUR or GBP using any available currency
3. Enter the amount and confirm
4. The transaction's confirmation details will appear if the transaction requires an exchange (if you selected a balance different from the currency you're adding)

## Top Up or Withdraw on Revolut X

1. Go to **'Home'** on the bottom menu
2. Select **'Deposit'** or **'Withdrawal'** in the centre
3. Once the option is selected, select **'Crypto'** or **'Cash'** on the pop-up
4. Enter the amount and confirm
5. The transaction's confirmation details will appear if the transaction requires an exchange (if you selected a balance different from the currency you're adding)
